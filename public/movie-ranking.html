The Best Movies According to Cindy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

It is an unforgettable, thrilling and enchanting anime movie.

Farewell My Concubine

A beautifully made film surrounding Chinese opera and traditional Chinese culture that brings audience back to "old society" China.

Titanic

Always remember "Where you go, I go" and the song "My heart will go on"
